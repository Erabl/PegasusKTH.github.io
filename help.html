What is PEGASEARCH?

PEGASEARCH is an easy tool for KTH students and staffs to search for prerequisites of a certain course.

How to use PEGASEARCH?

Enter the course code of the course you want to search prerequisites for in the search box on the homepage and
the prerequisites will be displayed both as a tree and as a list.

Is it only KTH students and staffs that can use this website?

No, our website is open for everyone but it is mostly useful for KTH students and staffs to check prerequisites for
specific courses. Other students who would like to know something about KTH courses are also welcome to use
our tool but only KTH courses can be found through this website.
